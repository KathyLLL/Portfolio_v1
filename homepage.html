About me

I am a digital designer focusing on interactive experiences and mobile apps, currently middle designer in Syndicode. I help clients grow their businesses by using design thinking to improve the experience of their customers. I create mobile apps, sites and brand identities.

 

I am really interested in travels. Only for this year I visited Katowice, Krakow, Warszawa, Prague, Paris, Rome, Naples, Sorrento, Capri. I was impressed by Louvre Museum and The Sistine Chapel.

 

I am open for new people and collaborations within brand identity, web design, motion, ux/ui design and 3d modeling.

For last 2 years I worked with clients from France, England, USA, Canada, Israel, Ukraine and Norway. It was fantastic experience.
